Frequently Asked Questions

Need answers? We’re here to help.

What is Silentum Retreat about? ▼
Silentum Retreat is a peaceful getaway focused on mindfulness, nature immersion, and silent reflection. Guests enjoy eco-cabins, guided meditation, silent dining, and nature-based experiences.
Is the retreat suitable for beginners? ▼
Absolutely! Whether you're new to meditation or simply need a peaceful break, our programs are beginner-friendly and staff are available to gently guide you.
What should I bring with me? ▼
We recommend comfortable clothing, a reusable water bottle, and any personal meditation accessories you may prefer. We provide essentials like bedding, meals, and toiletries.
Are meals included in the booking? ▼
Yes, all packages include plant-based silent dining prepared with locally sourced, seasonal ingredients.
Can I use my phone during the stay? ▼
We encourage a digital detox. Phones can be used privately in your cabin but are discouraged in shared and nature spaces to preserve silence and presence.
