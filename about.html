Overview

Silentum Retreat is a sanctuary designed for silence, deep rest, and reconnection with nature. Located in Awissawella, our off-grid, soundproof eco-cabins and minimalist luxury help guests unplug from digital life and reconnect with themselves.

Services & Facilities

  • Soundproof eco-cabins with aromatherapy
  • Digital detox experience (device surrender)
  • Sleep optimization sessions with certified sleep coaches
  • Plant-based meals served in silent zones
  • Yoga, meditation, journaling & forest walks
  • Mindful crafting, breathwork, and gratitude rituals
